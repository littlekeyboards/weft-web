What Weft does

Wardrobe

Photograph and catalogue every item you own. Track colors, fits, brands, and condition—all in one place.

Style Guide

Pick an item and get outfit ideas built around it, tailored to your style and what you already own.

Fit Check

Upload a photo of your outfit and get an honest, constructive critique—from overall impression to specific styling notes.

Scout

Photograph something you're considering buying. Find out whether it fits your wardrobe before you commit.
